About

THE DON

Levar Tha Don is one of Tuniverse Inc.'s standout vocalists, bringing a distinctive style that blends soulful R&B, hip-hop, and pop influences. His voice carries weight — every lyric feels lived-in and real.

With productions backed by VA Allstarz, Levar delivers tracks that resonate far beyond the studio. His ability to connect with listeners through storytelling sets him apart in a crowded landscape.

Catch Levar Tha Don on all major streaming platforms and stay tuned for upcoming releases through Tuniverse Inc.
